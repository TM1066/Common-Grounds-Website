Info

Situated in the historic centre of Lincoln, Common Grounds is located only a stone’s throw away from the Cathedral. In a parade of shops just off Steep Hill, the building has roots stretching all the way back to Lincoln’s Roman past.

The charm of Steep Hill and its connected streets has not gone unnoticed. It has been awarded numerous awards and accolades over the years. Most recently, it was awarded the Historic Centre award in 2022.

Situated in a building which has been dated to medieval times, the surroundings and ambience of Coffee Grounds certainly adds to the feeling of being in Lincoln’s Cathedral Quarter.

When Is The Shop Quietest?
We value our customers focus heavily, and appreciate that some people may be more comfortable coming to the shop at a time when its easier to tune out excess noise
In accordance to this, we dedicate 3-5pm as a 'quiet time' where we ask all customers to keep noise to a minimum!
